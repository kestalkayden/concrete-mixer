Tired of placing concrete powder next to water, scooping the result, and repeating?

The **Concrete Mixer** is a single-block solution that crafts colored concrete blocks furnace-style. No power required, no setup, just feed it materials and water.

## Two crafting paths

**Raw recipe** — mimics vanilla concrete crafting in one machine:
- 4 sand + 4 gravel + 1 dye + 1000 mB water → 4 concrete blocks (12 seconds)
- Inputs go in any of the 3 input slots, any order

**Powder shortcut** — for when you already have concrete powder:
- 1 concrete powder + 250 mB water → 1 concrete block (3 seconds)
- Matches vanilla intuition — 1 powder + water = 1 concrete

## Features

- **10-bucket water tank** (10,000 mB) — fill via vanilla buckets OR any mod's fluid-storing item (Tech Reborn cells, Mekanism portable tanks, etc.)
- **Fluid pipe compatibility** — any pipe mod using standard fluid capabilities (Pipez, Create, Mekanism, EnderIO, etc.) can pump water into the tank
- **Jade / WAILA compatibility** — hover the block to see tank contents
- **Status indicators** — GUI shows Mixing / Idle / Needs water / Output full / Color mismatch / Disabled (redstone) so you always know what the mixer is doing
- **Hopper compatibility** — push inputs from the top/sides, pull output from the bottom, refill buckets from the sides
- **Color-lock output** — output slot accepts only one color at a time, so you can leave a mixer mid-batch without worrying about color mixing
- **Redstone control** — apply a signal to pause; remove to resume (progress preserved)
- **Single tier, no-power** design — craftable mid-game and immediately useful
- **Contents preserved on break** — break a loaded mixer with a pickaxe, re-place, all inventory intact

## Recipe

8 iron ingots ringing a cauldron in a crafting table → 1 Concrete Mixer.

```
[Iron][Iron][Iron]
[Iron][Cauldron][Iron]
[Iron][Iron][Iron]
```
